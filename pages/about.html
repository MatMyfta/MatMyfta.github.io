About me


I was born in Elbasan (AL) in 1998, until my family and I went to Italy, when I still was very young, and unfortunately now I don't go anymore in Albania very often. In Italy we lived in Tuscany for a long time with a large part of my relatives, such as cousins and uncles. But for family reasons we moved to South Tirol -- my current location.

When I was eight, I bought my first PC and - like all the kids in 2006 - I was the most excited kid when I realized my first artwork with paint. After a while I had finally even a internet connection, so I could get close and be fascinated by the internet world - an entire universe of informations and... games - the main reason I started my career in Google searching. Thanks to Google I found a lot of sites, that made me get close to web site development and graphic design in the near future - starting from free forum hosting - and there it is where I started making web layouts and coding with HTML and CSS.

Coding with HTML and CSS was my first step in my coding experience, and one of the most important in my life - actually the one that made me choose the Computer Science High School. In this High School my actual passion for programming and algorithms has started. In the beginning, I was programming with C++, then follow Java, SQL and PHP. Meanwhile I enveloped my skills in graphic design by myself.

After that I choose to go on with the studies, and I applied to the Department of Engineering and Science of Information in the University of Trento - where I continue study Computer Science.
