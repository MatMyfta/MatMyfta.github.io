About me

I'm a Computer Scientist (or at least I like to believe that) and a noobie Graphic Designer with (not so) several hobbies.

I started my experience in ~2010 when I started using Gimp in order to create personalized Facebook covers and simple pics. Further on I learned Photoshop and its extremely wide usability. After that, I extended my horizons by creating Web Layouts in addition to simple photo manipulations.

This move brought me to learn coding, starting from HTML and CSS, then I continued my studies in programming by choosing computer science high school. High school made me curious in much more fields of Computer Science, such as programming, computer networks and algorithms.

This curiosity made me choose to continue my studies and attend the Computer Science faculty in the University of Trento.
